Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common parent queries

We provide daily updates through our Parent Portal and mobile app. Teachers upload photos, activity summaries, and learning observations for each child. You'll receive notifications when new updates are posted. For Playgroup and Nursery students, we also maintain a physical communication diary that comes home daily.

Please inform the school office before 8:30 AM by calling our absence reporting line at +91 98765-43211 or marking attendance in the Parent Portal. For illnesses lasting more than two days, please provide a doctor's note upon return. If your child has a contagious condition, please inform us immediately so we can take necessary precautions.

You can schedule meetings with teachers through the Parent Portal by selecting "Request Meeting" under the "Communication" tab. Alternatively, you can email the teacher directly or call the school office. Teachers are available for brief conversations during drop-off and pick-up times, but we recommend scheduling a proper meeting for in-depth discussions.

Your child should bring:
  • School bag with communication diary
  • Water bottle (labeled with name)
  • Change of clothes in a sealed bag
  • Lunch box if not opting for school meals
  • Any prescribed medication (to be handed directly to the teacher)
Please ensure all items are clearly labeled with your child's name.

We encourage parents to reinforce learning at home by:
  • Reading with your child daily for at least 15 minutes
  • Engaging in conversations about their school day
  • Using the at-home activities provided in our weekly newsletter
  • Creating a consistent routine for homework and play
  • Attending parent workshops to learn specific supporting strategies
Our "Learning at Home" section in the Parent Zone provides additional resources and activities aligned with our curriculum.
