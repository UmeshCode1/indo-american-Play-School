Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our admissions process

We recommend applying at least 3-6 months before the academic session begins. Our admissions for the upcoming academic year 2025-26 are open from October 2024. Early applications have a better chance of securing a spot as we admit students on a first-come-first-served basis once all criteria are met.

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • 4 passport size photographs of the child
  • 2 passport size photographs of each parent/guardian
  • Proof of residence (Aadhar Card/Utility Bill/Rental Agreement)
  • Immunization record
  • Previous school records (if applicable)

We do not conduct formal entrance tests for pre-primary grades. Instead, we have a friendly interaction session with the child and parents to understand the child's developmental needs and to ensure that our educational approach aligns with your expectations. This session helps us determine how we can best support your child's learning journey.

Yes, we provide transportation services covering most areas around the school. Our buses are equipped with GPS tracking systems, trained attendants, and safety features. Transportation fees vary based on distance, and you can inquire about specific routes during the admission process.

Our school timing varies by program:
  • Playgroup: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Nursery: 9:00 AM - 12:30 PM
  • LKG & UKG: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
We also offer extended daycare facilities until 3:30 PM for working parents.
