Creating the Perfect Environment for Learning

Our facilities are designed with children's safety, comfort, and developmental needs in mind

At Indo American Play School, we believe that the environment plays a crucial role in a child's learning and development. Our thoughtfully designed facilities create a safe, stimulating, and nurturing space where children can explore, discover, and grow.

Every aspect of our campus has been planned with attention to detail, from the vibrant colors that stimulate creativity to the child-sized furniture that promotes independence and comfort.

Our facilities meet and exceed all safety standards, ensuring that parents can have peace of mind while their children learn and play in our care.

Key Features
  • Child-friendly infrastructure
  • Age-appropriate learning zones
  • CCTV surveillance
  • Sanitized environments
  • Earthquake-resistant building
  • Fire safety measures
  • Well-ventilated spaces
  • Eco-friendly campus

Safety First

Your child's safety is our highest priority

CCTV Surveillance

Our entire campus is monitored by CCTV cameras that cover all areas including classrooms, corridors, play areas, and entrances. This ensures constant supervision and security.

Fire Safety

Our facilities are equipped with fire detection systems, extinguishers, and clearly marked evacuation routes. Regular fire drills are conducted to ensure everyone knows safety procedures.

Secure Access

Our school has a single point entry system with security personnel. Visitors must sign in and out, and children are released only to authorized individuals with proper identification.

Medical Readiness

We maintain a well-equipped first aid room and have staff trained in pediatric first aid and CPR. We also have partnerships with nearby medical facilities for emergencies.

Child-Safe Design

Our facilities feature rounded edges, non-toxic materials, child-friendly furniture, and soft impact surfaces under play equipment to prevent injuries.

Health & Hygiene

We implement strict cleaning protocols, regular sanitization, air purifiers, and health checks to maintain a hygienic environment and prevent the spread of illness.
