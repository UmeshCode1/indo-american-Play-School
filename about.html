Our Story

Indo American Play School was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a nurturing educational environment that combines the best of American teaching methodologies with traditional Indian values.

What started as a small playgroup with just 15 children has now grown into a comprehensive early childhood education center serving over 200 families from diverse cultural backgrounds.

Our journey has been guided by our commitment to providing children with a strong foundation that prepares them for future academic success while nurturing their cultural identity and global awareness.

Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of the leading preschools in the region, known for our innovative curriculum, passionate educators, and supportive learning environment.

Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach

Our Vision

To nurture globally conscious young minds who are rooted in cultural values while being prepared for future challenges and opportunities in an interconnected world.

  • Creating lifelong learners with curious minds
  • Fostering respect for diversity and cultural heritage
  • Developing confidence, creativity, and critical thinking
  • Building strong foundations for academic excellence

Our Mission

To provide a joyful, stimulating, and safe learning environment where children develop holistically through a balanced curriculum that integrates American educational innovation with Indian cultural wisdom.

  • Implementing play-based, experiential learning methodologies
  • Nurturing social-emotional development alongside academic skills
  • Celebrating cultural diversity and fostering global citizenship
  • Partnering with parents as co-educators in children's development

Our Educational Philosophy

At Indo American Play School, we believe that every child is unique and deserves an educational approach that honors their individual learning style, interests, and developmental pace.

Our educational philosophy is built on the following principles:

Child-Centered Approach

We place children at the center of the learning process, allowing their curiosity and interests to guide educational experiences.

Holistic Development

We focus on cognitive, physical, social, emotional, and spiritual development, recognizing that all aspects of growth are interconnected.

Play-Based Learning

We understand that play is children's natural way of exploring, experimenting, and making sense of the world around them.

Cultural Integration

We blend American educational approaches with Indian cultural values to create a unique learning experience that celebrates diversity.
